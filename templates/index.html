About Our Clinic

Charitable dental clinic is a non-profit dental clinic dedicated to serving individuals and families who lack access to adequate dental care. Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate volunteer dentists, our mission is to eliminate dental pain and restore dignity through comprehensive oral healthcare.

We operate solely on donations and the dedication of our volunteer staff. Every patient is treated with the utmost respect and care, regardless of their ability to pay.

Our Dental Services

We provide a wide range of essential dental treatments to ensure your oral health is at its best.

Regular Check-up

Comprehensive oral examinations to detect and prevent dental issues early.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar, ensuring healthy gums and teeth.

Tooth Extraction

Safe and painless removal of severely decayed or damaged teeth.

Dental X-Rays

Advanced diagnostic imaging to see beneath the surface of your teeth and gums.

Fillings & Restorations

Restoring the function and integrity of missing tooth structure.

Orthodontic Consultation

Basic consultations for braces and alignment corrections for children and adults.
